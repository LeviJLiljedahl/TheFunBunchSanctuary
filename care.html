The Fun Bunch Sanctuary - Taking Care of Your Pets

One of the first steps to improving animal welfare can take place right in your own home. Perhaps you are thinking about getting an animal companion or already have one. Well, the best thing you can do is to educate yourself about how to best take care of them.

For instance, be sure to give your furry friend a healthy amount of food each day. Learn the recommended amount based on their species, their size, and their age. Also be sure that your animal friend always has access to fresh drinking water.

It is also important to provide your friend shelter from the elements. Even if they don't stay inside with you, make sure they have some way to stay cool in the summer heat, warm in the cold of winter, dry from the rain, and protected from the wind or other elements. A good tip is that if you are hot or cold outside, an animal will be too!

For more tips on how to take care of your pet, check out the links below!

  • Animal Foundation: The Basic Necessities of Proper Pet Care
  • Ross University: Guide For Taking Care of Animals
  • ASPCA: Pet Care
  • PetMD: The Responsible Pet Owner's Checklist for Taking Care of a Pet
  • St. George's University: Taking Care of Animals: Pro Tips for Pet Owner's
  • PETA: Caring for Animal Companions

These are just a few links that contain a lot of good information. Feel free to do a search for more resources on your own. The more educated you can be, the better equiped you will be to help, not only your pet, but all animals.

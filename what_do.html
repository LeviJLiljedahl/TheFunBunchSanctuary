The Fun Bunch Sanctuary - What We Do

What is The Fun Bunch Sanctuary

The Fun Bunch Sanctuary is 10 acres of property located in Chetek, WI but more importantly it is a safe place, an oasis, and a forever home for any animal who has been abandoned, neglected, abused, or simply is in need of a good home.

The "Fun Bunch"

The Fun Bunch, or the bunch, for short, is what we call all of those who inhabit the sanctuary, whether that be the animals or the humans. At the moment, the animals that are living at the sanctuary are made up of just cats and dogs, but over the years we have had many other species stay with us and the sanctuary is open to any animal in need.

What all do we do

The main purpose of The Fun Bunch Sanctuary is to give a forever home to animals in need, but the truth is we do a lot more, all with animal welfare in mind. Besides providing a safe place for animals to live, some of the other things we do include but are not limited to:

  • Respond to tips about animals being abused or neglected
  • Work with local shelters, the ASPCA, and other organizations with the goal of improving animal welfare.
  • Work with local Veterinary Clinics to help control over population issues, meaning spaying and neuturing cats and dogs
  • Helping others to find good homes for or relocating pets
  • Provide resources about taking better care of pets
  • And much more

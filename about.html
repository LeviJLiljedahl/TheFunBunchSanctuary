The Fun Bunch Sanctuary - About Us

How we Started

The Fun Bunch Sanctuary was officially founded by the Marquardt family in 2021, but the family has been taking in and caring for animals long before. It started with local cats and dogs who were abandoned and has grown from there. Located on 10 acres in Chetek, Wisconsin, The Fun Bunch Sanctuary is home to animals of all species. At the moment the "bunch", as they have become known as, is comprised of cats and dogs, but the sanctuary is open to any animal in need of a forever home. To learn more about what we do, feel free to click the following link, What We Do. If you would like to learn more about our mission, click the following link, Our Mission.

We first saw the need for a safe place for animals when the COVID-19 pandemic began. One of the first things that happened when people were required to stay at home, was that many people rushed out to humane societies across the U.S. and adopted cats, dogs, and other animal companions. This is great, who better to spend the day with than a furry friend. The problem was that when people began returning to work many animals were surrendered or abandoned due to lack of time. While it is better to give an animal to a proper shelter rather than just abandoning them, this can still be tough on the animal. We, meaning the founders of The Fun Bunch Sanctuary, realized that animals of all kinds need stability to be happy. That is when we really opened our doors and began our mission of improving the quality of life for as many critters as possible. Thus, The Fun Bunch and their Sanctuary was created!

Volunteers and What they Do!

At the Fun Bunch Sanctuary we rely very heavily on the kindness of fellow animal lovers and those willing to volunteer their time to help. Being a completely non-profit organization that does not sell any products or services, all of the supplies and anything else the animals need come directly out of our pockets or from the donations of generous patrons. As such, we are unable to afford any paid workers. Therefore, generous volunteers donate their time each week to help out and care for the animals. Volunteers each week will and may do any of the following:

  • Spend time with the animals
  • Take dogs for walks around the property
  • Play with the cats
  • Help clean up after the pets
  • Help maintain the property
  • Cuddle with any animals that want it
  • And much more

Volunteers are free to come and go as they please, as well as set their own hours. We have all volunteers sign in when they arrive and sign out when they leave, in order to keep track of all those who donate their time. Then, once a year, to show our appreciation, we throw a huge celebration and invite back all of the volunteers who have donated their time. This celebration includes food, drinks, fun, and games for all ages and all species!

We would like to extend a huge thank you to all those who volunteer and/or donate. What we do would not be possible without special people like you. From all of the animals and us humans at The Fun Bunch Sanctuary, we say Thank You!

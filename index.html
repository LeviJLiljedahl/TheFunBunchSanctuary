The Fun Bunch Sanctuary

Welcome to "The Bunch"!

Thank you for visiting our web page! The Fun Bunch Sanctuary is a safe haven for animals and humans alike. Officially founded in 2021, The Fun Bunch Sanctuary takes in all species of animals who are in need of a forever home. Whether their history is filled with neglect, abuse, or abandonment, every animal can feel happy and safe at The Fun Bunch Sanctuary!

Newest Addition to the Bunch

Binky is the newest member of our bunch but only on a technicality. He was actually the last born of a group of three kittens. His mother is Lily, a cat that was abandoned in the neighborhood. Soon, after bringing Lily in, we found out she was actually pregnant. She soon gave birth to Kirby, Willow, and last but not least Binky.

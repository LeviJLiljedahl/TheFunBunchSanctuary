The Fun Bunch Sanctuary - Our Mission

The Fun Bunch Santuary's mission is simple, to improve the lives of as many animals as we can. We can accomplish this not only by taking in animals who have been abused, neglected, or abandoned and giving them a forever home right here at the sanctuary, but by working with and educating others to help improve animal welfare as well.

Unfortunately, there are still many people out there that see animals as lesser than or simply as property to be used in any way that they see fit. Our mission is to help the world to realize that all animals should be treated with the respect that they deserve. That is why, here at the sanctuary, we don't consider "the bunch" to be pets but we consider them to be our family and we treat them with the same respect that we would treat any other human member of our family.

By working with other individuals and organizations we hope to improve animal welfare, not only in our local area, but across the United States and perhaps one day the world.

You can help to make a difference by continuing to treat the animals in your life with respect and by continuing to educate yourself on how to best care for them. You can also make a difference by spreading your knowledge throughout your community and by helping any animal you see in need.
